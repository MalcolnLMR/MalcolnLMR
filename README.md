### My last Character Development Arc - "Arch Linux"
Learning Arch follows the steps from the Hero Journey. Starting as 'omg I dont wanna go to win11 :sob:', then I went to Ubuntu, didnt like gnome and wanted to try KDE (but I didnt knew I could just try to change my DE or WM). So I stumbled on someone saying 'I use arch btw' as a meme. BUUUT, that triggered me to try it. ooooh god, why? In a nutshell, I wanted to do it the 'hard' way, installing the distro all by myself, no scripts or arch install. Just me and the terminal (also the wiki). After 2 days of being unable to log into my own pc (I messed up) I finally got it.
